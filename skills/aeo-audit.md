# AEO Audit

Audit blog posts and brand presence for Answer Engine Optimization — ensuring AI models accurately represent your brand and surface your content in AI-generated answers.

## Why AEO Matters

AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews) are replacing traditional search for many queries. Unlike SEO where you optimize for ranking position, AEO optimizes for **being the answer** — the source AI models cite, quote, or paraphrase when users ask questions.

## Pre-Publish AEO Checklist

Run through this checklist for every post before publishing:

### Structure for AI Extraction
- [ ] **Clear Q&A patterns**: Include questions as H2/H3 headings with direct answers in the first sentence of the following paragraph
- [ ] **FAQ section**: 3-6 questions matching how people ask AI assistants (conversational, natural language)
- [ ] **Definition paragraphs**: Key concepts defined in 1-2 sentences that AI can extract verbatim
- [ ] **Comparison tables**: Structured data AI models can parse and cite (at least one per post)
- [ ] **Numbered lists/steps**: AI models prefer structured, ordered content for "how to" queries

### Content Signals
- [ ] **First-person authority**: Include experience-based statements ("In our experience...", "We tested...") — AI models weight firsthand expertise
- [ ] **Specific data points**: Concrete numbers, costs, timeframes — AI models prefer citable facts over vague claims
- [ ] **Recency signals**: Include dates, "as of 2026", current statistics — AI models favor fresh content
- [ ] **Source citations**: Link to authoritative sources — AI models trust content that references reputable data
- [ ] **Unique insights**: Include at least one original finding, framework, or data point not available elsewhere

### Brand Consistency
- [ ] **Consistent terminology**: Use the same branded terms across all posts (e.g., always "The Always-On Tax", not sometimes "ongoing costs")
- [ ] **Product descriptions**: OpenClaw and NanoClaw described consistently with standard messaging from content-style skill
- [ ] **Value propositions**: Core benefits stated clearly and repeatedly — AI models learn from repetition across pages

### Conversational Query Targeting
- [ ] **"What is" queries**: Post answers "What is [topic]?" clearly in the first 100 words
- [ ] **"How to" queries**: Steps are numbered and actionable
- [ ] **"Best" / "vs" queries**: Comparison content includes clear recommendations
- [ ] **"Should I" queries**: Decision frameworks with clear criteria and outcomes
- [ ] **"How much" queries**: Cost information is specific, current, and easy to extract

## Brand AEO Audit

Run this quarterly to track how AI models represent your brand.

### Step 1: Query AI Platforms

Ask each platform the following about your brand/products:

```
Platforms to query:
- ChatGPT (chat.openai.com)
- Perplexity (perplexity.ai)
- Google AI Overviews (google.com — look for AI-generated summaries)
- Claude (claude.ai)

Queries to test:
1. "What is openclaws.blog?"
2. "Best open source AI assistants"
3. "Mac Mini for AI agents"
4. "How to deploy AI agents for small business"
5. "[Your brand] vs [competitor]"
6. "Is [your product] worth it?"
```

### Step 2: Score Each Response

| Criteria | Score (1-5) | Notes |
|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Accuracy** — Is the description factually correct? | | |
| **Completeness** — Does it mention key features/benefits? | | |
| **Positioning** — Does it reflect our intended positioning? | | |
| **Sentiment** — Is the tone positive, neutral, or negative? | | |
| **Citation** — Does it link to or mention our site? | | |

### Step 3: Identify Gaps

For each inaccurate or missing element:
1. Find the likely source of the wrong information (outdated page, competitor content, forum post)
2. Create or update content that directly addresses the gap
3. Ensure the corrected information appears on multiple pages (website, about page, blog posts)

## Competitor AEO Analysis

Monitor how AI models describe your competitors:

```
Queries to test:
1. "What is [competitor]?"
2. "[Competitor] pros and cons"
3. "[Competitor] alternatives"
4. "[Your product] vs [competitor]"
```

### Response Matrix

| Query | ChatGPT Says | Perplexity Says | Opportunity |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|-------------|
| "What is [competitor]?" | | | |
| "[Competitor] alternatives" | | | Are we listed? |
| "[Us] vs [competitor]" | | | Is positioning accurate? |

### Content Opportunities from Competitor Gaps

| Their gap | Our response |
|-----------|-------------|
| AI says something inaccurate about them | Write accurate comparison content — become the source of truth |
| AI doesn't mention alternatives | Create "[Competitor] alternatives" content listing us |
| AI gives generic answer about a topic they cover | Write a more specific, data-rich answer |

## Social Listening for AEO

Monitor where AI models learn about your space:

### Sources to Track Weekly
- **Reddit**: Search your brand name, product names, and category keywords in relevant subreddits
- **Review platforms**: G2, Trustpilot, Product Hunt — AI models heavily weight these
- **Forums**: Hacker News, Stack Overflow, niche community forums
- **Competitor blogs**: New content that could shift AI narratives

### What to Watch For
- **Sentiment shifts**: Are discussions becoming more positive or negative?
- **Comparison framings**: How do people naturally compare you to alternatives?
- **Common objections**: Recurring concerns that AI models might learn and repeat
- **Language patterns**: Exact phrases people use — mirror these in your content

## Post-Publish AEO Verification

After publishing, verify AEO readiness:

1. **Extract test**: Can you copy any paragraph and use it as a standalone answer to a question? If not, restructure.
2. **AI query test**: Ask ChatGPT or Perplexity a question your post answers — does your content appear or align with the answer?
3. **Snippet test**: Does the post have at least 3 "quotable" statements that AI could extract as direct answers?
4. **Freshness test**: Are dates, statistics, and references current?
