# Customer Segmentation

## Primary Persona: Demand Gen Director at V2Cloud

### Profile

- **Title**: Director of Demand Generation
- **Company**: V2Cloud (v2cloud.com)
- **Location**: London, United Kingdom
- **Experience**: 15+ years digital marketing. Background in growth leadership, paid marketing, and digital strategy across multiple companies
- **Side activity**: Digital marketing mentor on MentorCruise

### What They Own

- Blog content strategy and publishing cadence
- SEO pipeline and organic traffic growth
- Demand generation (inbound leads, content-driven pipeline)
- Reports to CEO or Director of Product Marketing

### Their Team

| Role | Notes |
|------|-------|
| **Lead content writer** (likely freelance/contract) | Based in Greece, runs her own content marketing agency. Wrote the Q3 2025 product updates, AI agents VM post |
| **Director of Product Marketing** | Based in Belarus. Handles product positioning, works closely with demand gen on messaging |
| **Partnership Program Manager** | Former MSP business owner, writes partner-focused blog content |
| **3-4 additional blog contributors** | Mix of staff and freelance writers |

### Their Priorities (Inferred from Site Activity)

1. **AI agent positioning** — Two major blog posts (Feb 2026) positioning V2Cloud VMs as AI agent infrastructure + new v2cloud.ai landing page. This is their big content bet for 2026.
2. **SEO refresh sprint** — 10+ blog posts refreshed in early March 2026, updating lastmod dates across engineering, GPU, and VDI content
3. **Content volume** — Needs more posts than the team can produce. Mix of in-house (demand gen director writes some) and contractors
4. **Industry verticals** — Engineering, healthcare, finance, education, BPO — each has a dedicated solutions page and needs content
5. **Partner enablement** — MSP and ISV partner programs need content support (partnership manager handles this)

### Their Pain Points

- **Capacity gap**: Small content team (1-2 writers + freelancers) producing 3-4 posts/month, needs 8+ to cover all verticals and the AI agent push
- **Freelancer management**: Lead content writer is a contractor in Greece — timezone gaps, voice consistency challenges
- **AEO blind spot**: V2Cloud's blog is optimized for traditional SEO but not for AI-generated answers. Their posts don't use the structural patterns that get cited by ChatGPT/Perplexity
- **New product launch**: v2cloud.ai is brand new — needs thought leadership content to establish authority in "VMs for AI agents" before competitors claim the space
- **Budget pressure**: ~$6.4M revenue company, bootstrapped/minimally funded. Every content dollar must justify itself

### What They Would Buy From Us

1. **Overflow content** — 4-6 additional posts/month that match V2Cloud's voice and cover their target verticals
2. **AEO optimization** — Audit and restructure existing posts so AI agents cite V2Cloud in answers
3. **AI agent thought leadership** — Co-authored pieces positioning V2Cloud as the infrastructure layer for business AI agents
4. **Content velocity** — Reliable publishing cadence without the freelancer management overhead
