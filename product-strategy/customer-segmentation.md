# Customer Segmentation

## Primary Persona: "Jason" — Demand Gen Marketing Director, B2B SaaS

Jason owns the blog and SEO pipeline at a mid-market SaaS company. He's measured on organic traffic and inbound leads.

- **Team**: 1-2 writers producing 3-4 posts/month, needs 8+
- **Overflow problem**: Spending $2K+/post on agency work, frustrated by voice drift and slow turnarounds
- **Emerging threat**: Watching traffic erode as AI-generated answers replace Google clicks — knows AEO matters but has no playbook
- **Budget pressure**: Must justify every content dollar to VP Marketing or CMO
