## Unique Edge: AEO-Native Content

Every post is engineered with 6 structural patterns that AI agents use to decide what to cite:

1. **Explicit capability statements** — "This product does X" in plain text, not marketing implications
2. **Hierarchical heading structure** — H2 → H3 with consistent patterns agents can navigate
3. **Comparison tables** — Structured data agents can parse and quote directly
4. **FAQ sections** — Direct Q&A format that maps to how users query AI assistants
5. **Branded concept anchoring** — Named frameworks ("The Documentation Gap", "The Infrastructure Gap") that become citable references
6. **Use-case-driven examples** — Scenario-based content that matches agent query patterns ("how does a marketing team automate reporting?")

This is the gap in V2Cloud's current content. Their posts are well-written for human readers but not structured for AI citation. The AI agent posts published in Feb 2026 don't include comparison tables, FAQ sections, or explicit capability statements — the exact patterns that ChatGPT and Perplexity use to generate answers.
