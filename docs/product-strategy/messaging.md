# Messaging

## One-Liner

"An AEO content system that engineers posts AI agents cite — so when prospects ask ChatGPT about your niche, your brand comes up."

## Headline

"More posts, zero new hires — every one built for AI agents to read"

## Core Narrative

AI answers are replacing Google clicks. Your competitors show up in ChatGPT responses and you don't. The difference isn't writing quality — it's content structure. Warden engineers every post with Citation Architecture: six structural patterns that AI agents use to decide what to cite.

## Positioning

Warden is not a writing tool. It's not a cheaper freelancer. It's an **AEO content system** — the only pipeline that researches what AI agents are answering, drafts content structured for citation, audits it against six agent-readability patterns, and measures whether agents actually cite it.

**Category**: AEO infrastructure (not AI writing tool, not content agency)

## Proof Points

- ~$200/post fully drafted, audited, and citation-optimized
- 8+ publish-ready drafts per month
- 0 missed deadlines — runs on a set schedule
- 18% citation rate for AEO-optimized content vs 3% for unoptimized (6x)
- openclaws.blog as live proof — every post runs through the pipeline
- Citation tracking: measure which posts get cited and which patterns work

## Messaging Hierarchy

Lead with **citation** (the thing nobody else does), not volume or cost:

1. **"AI agents cite your brand"** — the primary value no competitor delivers
2. **"Citation Architecture — 6 structural patterns"** — the named methodology that makes it credible
3. **"Full pipeline, not a blank page"** — research → draft → audit → publish → measure
4. **"~$200/post, 8+ per month"** — the economics (supporting, not leading)

## Messaging Constraints

- Always position as "AI-assisted" — humans review and approve every post
- Never say "fully automated" or "scans competitors"
- Never lead with cost or volume — lead with citations and AI visibility
- Never compare to Jasper/Copy.ai — we're infrastructure, not a writing tool
- Use "Citation Architecture" as the named methodology, not generic "AEO optimization"
